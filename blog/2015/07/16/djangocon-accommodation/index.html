DjangoCon Accommodation

DjangoCon Accommodation

July 16, 2015 by Anna Ossowski

DjangoCon US 2015 is only a few weeks away! Have you booked your accommodation yet?

We have negotiated a special rate with the conference center hotel for you and hope that you will take advantage of our full date range. Our rate for a single or double occupancy room is $139, discounted from $259. For more details about the hotel and booking information please visit our website.

Discounted conference rates are only available through August 17th, so please don’t wait any longer and book your room now!

We created a wiki page for those of you who would like to share a room and are still looking for a roommate. Please take advantage of this page!

We hope to see you in Austin in September!
